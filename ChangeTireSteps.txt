How To Change Tires

1. Grab your jack, spare tire, and tools.
2. Loosen the lug nuts slightly before you jack up the car.
3. Use the jack to lift the wheel off of the ground.
4. Remove the lug nuts and the wheel.
5. Remove the flat tire.
6. Put on your spare.
7. Tighten your lug nuts.
8. Lower your car.
9. Tighten your lug nuts as much as possible.
